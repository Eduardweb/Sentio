SentioQC

Next generation of Predictive Analytics simplified

Overview

SentioQC is a new predictive coding software that could be applied to any review (in process or new) without changing or disrupting current workflow. It is a product that could be applied without requiring the customer to commit to using AI technologies, reducing the perceived risks of using an unknown methodology.

SentioQC is a solution that provides a clear value to reduce risk and add functionality not available using other methods. It enables

Quality control for manual review

Ability to identify and correct reviews

Near real-time evaluation of reviewers

Near real-time evaluation of the overall review.

SentioQC uses machine learning to create a model for each issue tag. Once the models are created, SentioQC applies the models to documents that have been reviewed manually. In Relativity SentioQC creates batches of documents that may have been incorrectly tagged, and delivers a list of these documents as well as reports. Users have ability to verify the documents listed and make corrections, of necessary. The process can continue as long as users prefer. SentioQC can also provide results evaluating both reviewers and the overall review.

SentioQC is integrated with Relativity.
